About Us

Welcome to Dapoer Ecing!

Established in 2020, Dapoer Ecing is located in Cibubur, East Jakarta. We are a place where culinary delights meet the warmth of family. With a passion for serving delicious and high-quality dishes, Dapoer Ecing aims to indulge your taste buds with a variety of authentic and mouth-watering menu options.

We believe that food is one of the best ways to bring people together. That's why we strive to create a cozy and friendly atmosphere for every visitor who walks through our doors. From fresh ingredients to cooking with love, every detail at Dapoer Ecing is thoughtfully considered to ensure your satisfaction.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We hope to continue serving happiness through every dish we present.

Enjoy your meal and see you at Dapoer Ecing!
